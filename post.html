Book Recomendations of the Month

Here are some books to read this month

Updated April, 2024

Everyone needs a good book, and sometimes it is hard to find one that are both clean and fun to read. Here are 6 good books that you can read this month. Enjoy!



Fiction

Book Title

Author

Raiting

Why
The Sheriffs of Savage Wells Sarah M Eden 9/10 The town needs a new sheriff. Three volunteer and 3 wont back down.
The Understudy Jack Weyland 9/10 Easy read, cute story about an actor who is chosen to play the part of Jesus Christ in a movie, and as he learns about Christ, the man begins to change.
Out of My Mind Sarah M. Draper 11/10 Easy read, heartbreaking story of an incredibly smart 11 year old girl with cerebral palsy. She cannot speak or communicate and is trapped in her own mind until she gets a device that allows her to communicate.

Never Split The Difference Chris Voss 10/10 He teaches the art of negotiation. He shares stories from his experiences In the FBI, where negotiation is a matter of life or death. He shares principles that are cery applicable to everyday life.
9 Things You Simply Must Do Dr. Henry Cloud 8/10 Teaches principles for life. He comes from a Christian perspective and much of what he said has had an inpact on my life.
Boundaries Dr. Henry Cloud 10/10 Dr. Cloud teaches the importance of having boundaries. This book teaches when to say yes and how to say no.
